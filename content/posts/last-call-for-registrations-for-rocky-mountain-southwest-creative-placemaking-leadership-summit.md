There are only about 75 tickets left for the Rocky Mountain/Southwest Creative Placemaking Leadership Summit, April 6 and 7 at the University of Denver in Denver, Colorado. Registration is online at [https://www.cplsummit.org/southwest.](https://www.cplsummit.org/southwest)

The Rocky Mountain/Southwest summit, sponsored by the [National Consortium for Creative Placemaking](https://www.artsbuildcommunities.com/) and [ArtPlace America](https://www.artplaceamerica.org/) of the National Endowment for the Arts, is one of our four others that are taking place around the United States in 2018.  Other sites include Charleston, West Virginia, Chattanooga, Tennessee, and the New York-New Jersey area.

The Rocky Mountain/Southwest program focused on arts and entrepreneurship as well as creative placemaking in borderlands, mountain towns and Latino communities.  Check out the “Look Who’s Coming” section to see the wide variety of organizations and public agencies that will be represented there.

As an immediate incentive, last-minute registrants will receive a 10% coupon until all tickets are sold.  When registering, use discount code: *partner2018*All workshops, roundtables and knowledge exchanges in the Leadership Summit are being submitted for AICP Certification Maintenance credits.
